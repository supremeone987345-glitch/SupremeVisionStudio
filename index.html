About Me

Personal Story

For the past 1 year, I've been working with creators in the movie reaction niche, helping them transform raw recordings into polished, story-driven videos. My focus is not just on cutting and trimming, but on building the flow of the movie so that even first-time viewers stay hooked and enjoy the entire experience.
